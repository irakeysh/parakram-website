AboutPARAKRAM’16

The annual sports fest of ISM Dhanbad is a celebration of sporting talent and valor as the name goes. Offering a grand platform to the students of various educational institutes,it is the opportunity of a lifetime to be able to prove one's might in this extravaganza. The festival comprises of numerous events to leave no sporting talent untested or untouched. Motivational talks , gymnastic shows and quizzes are added spices to the real mix of sporting action of cricket, football, basketball and other events together with the grandeur of athletics and grace of lawn tennis , badminton's fire and speed to mention a few. The "Parakram" management team ensures that there will be efficient utilization of infrastructure with the entire ISM family of staff, faculty along with the student community taking part actively in the organization and success of the event.
